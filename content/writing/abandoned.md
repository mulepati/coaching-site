At the core of many of us
There is a little one
Shivering and scared
Holding so much pain

Born in a world that was too much
Where being wasn't enough
Where loving wasn't there

The little ones learned
To live with their fate
Just with a few tricks

Some learned to leave
Into others or into space
Hoping the body will take care of itself

Some learned to hide
Underneath them or with a mask
Hoping that one day they can just be

Some learned to fight
They embraced their fate
Believing in no one but themselves

With all the neat tricks
The pain was hidden away
They buried themselves
With a pile of shame

Awaiting a day
When they can be met once again
